About Us

Starry Night Café opened its doors in May of 2000. It is situated within a collection of historic buildings, including a covered bridge, in a lovely countryside setting. The building housing the restaurant was originally a cider press that was relocated to the site from southern Vermont and front dining room with its antique bar. The wooden leaves decorating the framework of the bar each depict the leaf of the tree


Starry Night's spacious octagonal dining room with picturesque views of the grounds was added in 2001 and quickly became a favorite space for intimate dining as well as larger private events. the addition of the Fireplace Room, and extensive enhancements to the grounds. Starry Night Café brings you an extraordinary experience.
